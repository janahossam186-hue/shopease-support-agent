# ShopEase Shipping Policy

## Overview
ShopEase is committed to delivering your orders quickly, safely, and affordably. This policy covers all shipments within the continental United States, Alaska, Hawaii, and internationally.

---

## Domestic Shipping Options

| Service | Delivery Time | Cost |
|---------|--------------|------|
| Standard Shipping | 5–7 business days | Free on orders ≥ $50; $5.99 otherwise |
| Express Shipping | 2–3 business days | $12.99 |
| Overnight Shipping | Next business day | $24.99 |
| Same-Day Delivery | Same day (select cities) | $14.99 |

*Business days: Monday–Friday, excluding federal holidays.*

---

## Free Shipping Threshold
- Standard shipping is **free** for all domestic orders totalling **$50 or more** after discounts.
- Free shipping applies at checkout automatically — no code required.
- Free shipping does not apply to oversized/freight items (noted on the product page).

---

## Order Processing
- Orders placed **before 2:00 PM EST** on business days are typically processed the same day.
- Orders placed after 2:00 PM EST, on weekends, or on holidays are processed the next business day.
- Processing time is **1–2 business days** for most items. Electronics and high-demand items may take up to 3 business days.
- You will receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking number once your order ships.

---

## Tracking
- Tracking information is provided via email and is viewable in your account under "My Orders."
- Tracking updates may take up to **24 hours** after shipment to appear on the carrier's website.
- Carriers used: UPS, FedEx, USPS, and regional carriers.

---

## International Shipping
- We ship to **40+ countries** worldwide.
- International delivery: **7–14 business days** (varies by destination).
- International shipping cost is calculated at checkout based on destination and package weight.
- **Import duties, taxes, and customs fees** are the customer's responsibility and are not included in our prices.
- Some products (electronics, batteries, oversized items) cannot be shipped to certain countries due to import restrictions.

---

## Undeliverable Packages
- If a package is returned to us due to an incorrect address, refusal, or failed delivery attempts:
  - We will notify you by email.
  - You can request re-shipment (additional shipping fee applies) or a refund minus the original shipping cost.
- **Address changes** after shipment are not possible. Please double-check your address at checkout.

---

## Lost or Damaged Packages
- If your package is lost in transit, contact us within **30 days** of the estimated delivery date.
- If your package arrives damaged, report it within **48 hours** of delivery with photos.
- We will file a carrier claim and either replace the item or issue a full refund.
- Investigations typically resolve within **5–7 business days**.

---

## Oversized & Freight Items
- Items over 150 lbs or with dimensions exceeding carrier limits ship via freight carriers.
- Freight shipping: **7–10 business days**; costs are quoted at checkout.
- Freight items require a delivery appointment. Ensure someone is available to receive the shipment.

---

## PO Boxes & APO/FPO Addresses
- USPS Standard Shipping is available for PO Boxes and APO/FPO addresses.
- Express and Overnight shipping are **not available** for PO Boxes or APO/FPO.

---

## Holiday Shipping
- During peak holiday periods (November–December), processing times may increase by 1–2 business days.
- Guaranteed delivery dates for holiday shipping will be posted on our website seasonally.
