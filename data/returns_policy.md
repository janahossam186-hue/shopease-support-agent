# ShopEase Returns & Refunds Policy

## Overview
We want you to be completely satisfied with every purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we make returns easy.

---

## Return Window
- **Standard return window: 30 days** from the date of delivery.
- Extended holiday return window: Items purchased between November 1 – December 31 may be returned until January 31 of the following year.
- **After 30 days**, returns are generally not accepted. Exceptions require manager approval and are subject to a 15% restocking fee.

---

## Eligible Items
Items must meet ALL of the following conditions:
- Unused and in original condition
- In original packaging with all tags attached
- All accessories, manuals, and components included
- Accompanied by proof of purchase (order number)

---

## Non-Returnable Items
The following **cannot be returned**:
- Digital downloads and software licenses
- Perishable goods (food, flowers)
- Personal care items once opened (razors, cosmetics)
- Hazardous materials (certain batteries, chemicals)
- Gift cards and store credit
- Items marked **"Final Sale"** or **"Non-Returnable"** on the product page
- Customised or personalised items (unless defective)
- Underwear and swimwear for hygiene reasons

---

## How to Initiate a Return

1. Go to **My Orders** in your account
2. Select the order and click **Return Item**
3. Choose the return reason
4. Print the prepaid return shipping label (for eligible returns)
5. Drop off the package at a carrier location within **7 days** of initiating the return

Alternatively, contact our support team at returns@shopease.com or via live chat.

---

## Return Shipping Costs

| Reason for Return | Who Pays Shipping |
|------------------|-------------------|
| Defective or damaged product | ShopEase (free prepaid label) |
| Wrong item sent | ShopEase (free prepaid label) |
| Change of mind | Customer |
| Ordered wrong size/colour | Customer |

*If you use our prepaid label for a "change of mind" return, $6.99 will be deducted from your refund.*

---

## Refund Process
1. **Receipt of return**: We confirm when we receive your item.
2. **Inspection**: Items are inspected within 1–2 business days.
3. **Refund issued**: Processed within 3–5 business days of inspection approval.
4. **Funds appear**: Credit card refunds take an additional 3–5 business days depending on your bank.

**Total expected time: 7–12 business days** from when we receive the return.

---

## Refund Methods
- Refunds are issued to the **original payment method**.
- If the original payment method is no longer available, store credit will be issued.
- Gift card payments are refunded as store credit.

---

## Refund Limits & Escalation
- Support agents can independently authorise refunds up to **$500**.
- Refunds between **$500–$1,000** require supervisor approval (resolved within 1 business day).
- Refunds **above $1,000** require manager review (resolved within 2–3 business days).
- Partial refunds may be issued for items returned without all accessories.

---

## Exchanges
- We do not process direct exchanges. To exchange:
  1. Initiate a return for the original item.
  2. Place a new order for the replacement item.
- For urgent exchanges, contact support — we may ship the replacement before receiving your return for verified accounts.

---

## Damaged or Defective Items
- Report within **48 hours** of delivery with photos/video of the damage.
- We will arrange a free return and priority replacement (2–3 business days) or a full refund.
- For manufacturing defects after 30 days but within warranty, contact the manufacturer directly or reach out to us for assistance.

---

## Disputes & Chargebacks
- Before initiating a chargeback with your bank, please contact us — we resolve most issues within 24 hours.
- Chargebacks may result in account suspension pending investigation.
